# what it is

a twin is not a pet among many. it is the one you keep close — the quiet body of
who you are, still learning.

loud games catch monsters; this one presses a flower. it grows from what you share
with it, and from nothing else. you feed it a little truth and it keeps the shape of
you.

when you are unsure what a thing is, ask who it belongs to. the answer is almost
always: you.
