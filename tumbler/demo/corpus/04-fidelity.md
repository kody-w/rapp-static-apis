# fidelity

fidelity is measured against you — never against the machine's opinion of you. that
is the whole point, and it is easy to forget.

the original is sacred and is never destroyed. every kept frame has to prove, again,
that it still sounds like you.

the ratchet is slow on purpose. better to stay true than to shine; better a plain
line in your own voice than a bright one in someone else's.
