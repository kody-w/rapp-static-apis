    Why it's cool

    A souvenir is usually frozen in time. This one stays alive. A quiet magnet on your fridge can still open a tiny real-time truth from far away — the air, the hour, whether the city is in daylight or under stars — without asking any private server to remember anything for you.

    Make your own · run it yourself

    1. Open /track and choose an Open-Meteo URL for your place's latitude and longitude.
    2. Keep one path simple (like current.temperature_2m) so track/run.html can show the same source live.
    3. Use Make the QR below to draw that run page URL with qr.mjs, then print it as your magnet.

    Local: python3 -m http.server in the repo root, then open localhost:8000/showcase/demos/the-far-window/ (crypto.subtle needs a secure context — localhost or https, not file://).
