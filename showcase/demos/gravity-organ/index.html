    Why it's cool

    It feels like pinball plus music sequencer: every collision is both a physics event and a note. Because gravity itself is live, the same setup becomes a different instrument as you lean the device.

    How to

    1. Press Drop a marble to start motion and sound immediately.
    2. Switch modes to paint Pegs (bouncy) and Bells (musical targets) onto the board.
    3. Tilt your phone to steer gravity. If no sensor is available, use arrow keys or tilt buttons.

    Local: python3 -m http.server in the repo root, then open localhost:8000/showcase/demos/gravity-organ/.
