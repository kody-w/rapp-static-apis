The Sound of Far Away

Live — run it right now

Pick a coastline, press play, and let it breathe. Your browser fetches live Open-Meteo wind + marine data through a pinned /track cell, verifies the bytes, then maps those values into soft surf, distant rumble, and occasional gusts.

— choose a coast and press play —

Watch what's happening

    Why it's cool

    It is not a dashboard. It is a presence. The data never touches a backend, and the browser does not show you a bunch of numbers — it turns what the coast is doing this minute into one restrained sound you can leave on in the room.

    Run it yourself

    Local: python3 -m http.server in the repo root, then open localhost:8000/showcase/demos/the-sound-of-far-away/ (crypto.subtle needs a secure context — localhost or https, not file://).
