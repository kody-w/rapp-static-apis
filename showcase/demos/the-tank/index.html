    Why it's cool

    It's a tiny social game with no app account and no backend. Every open issue labeled fish becomes one swimmer in this canvas tank, so your fish joins a crowd that keeps growing over time.

    How to

    1. Pick hue, fins, and wiggle.
    2. Press Start the tank to spawn your fish immediately.
    3. Press release yours, submit the prefilled GitHub issue, then refresh to see it rejoin from shared state.

    Tip: open with python3 -m http.server at repo root, then visit /showcase/demos/the-tank/.
