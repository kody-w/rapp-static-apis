    Why it's cool

    You never get the full context while drawing, so collaboration isn't coordinated — it's accidental. The final creature is a weird fossil of partial intentions, permanently reconstructed from issue-backed fragments with no backend.

    How to

    1. Press Start a blind handoff to load a random parent segment and reveal only its tail.
    2. Draw from the glowing start point in the right canvas.
    3. Press release your segment, submit the prefilled issue, and your piece becomes permanent shared state.

    Local: python3 -m http.server in repo root, then open /showcase/demos/nobody-drew-this-alone/.
