          Why it's cool

          The Democratic Market already proves an unkillable, notarized economy where every sale is gated by ECDSA-signed, append-only governance votes. The Command Center already proves a one-screen operator console of hash-verified live tiles. This weld keeps both intact and fuses them at the console: the city's governance verdicts, custody heads, and permanent-ledger state now paint tiles beside the verified outside-world feeds.

          The tiles are not decorative. The governance tile only turns green after the whole ballot chain replays and re-verifies (including a replay of persisted history on every boot). The ledger tile only turns green after every entry's parent pointer, content hash, freshness proof, and seller signature re-verify. Tamper with one byte of history and the same tiles turn REFUSED — one tab both watches and governs an autonomous civilization, with no backend.

          Run it yourself

          1. Press Boot the sovereign city. The console resolves the same pinned cell across dead and surviving mirrors, loads live sold-out labels from GitHub Issues, replays any persisted governance ledger, and refreshes the three verified external-feed tiles — all in one Promise.allSettled.
          2. Select at least one available item and press Run trustless governance poll. Six agents mint ECDSA ballots, race append-only consensus, and the governance tile paints only from the re-verified tally.
          3. Only after a YES majority ratifies the cart, type a seller passphrase and press Finalize governed order + append to permanent ledger.
          4. Watch the order become an RSL1 envelope, a custody link, a seller-signed receipt, and a permanent ledger entry that embeds the governance verdict — then watch the ledger tile go green only after the full chain replays.
          5. Press Tamper latest ledger entry to mutate stored content. Verification halts on that exact entry and the civic tiles flip to REFUSED.

          Local: python3 -m http.server in the repo root, then open localhost:8000/showcase/demos/the-sovereign-city/ (crypto.subtle needs localhost or https, not file://).
