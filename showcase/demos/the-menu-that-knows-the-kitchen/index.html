    Why it's cool

    A paper menu is supposed to be stale by definition. This one isn't: the kitchen updates stock by opening or closing GitHub Issues, and every scan resolves fresh state client-side. No backend to deploy, no dashboard to keep alive. The printed artifact survives forever while the data stays live.
