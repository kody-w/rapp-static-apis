    Why it's cool

    It feels like party-magic because the table is instantly playable without infrastructure: one click makes private seat cards, every player reveals only their own role, and nothing depends on a host, account system, or game server.

    How to

    1. Set the player count and press Deal.
    2. Let each person scan their seat's QR and enter their PIN.
    3. Everyone learns their role privately; discussion starts immediately.

    Local: python3 -m http.server at repo root, then open localhost:8000/showcase/demos/spy-at-the-table/ (localhost is treated as a secure context by browsers, so crypto.subtle works there, as on https).
