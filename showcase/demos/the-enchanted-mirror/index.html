    Why it's cool

    A style-transfer model would normally need a backend or heavy libraries. This version does the spell with just browser primitives: webcam pixels are downsampled, remapped into a palette sampled from a museum painting, then painted back as jittered brush-like strokes in real time. The “haunting” artwork is disclosed on-screen so the aesthetic source is explicit, and every frame lives and dies locally in your tab.

    Run it yourself

    1. Open this page over https or localhost and click Start the mirror.
    2. Grant camera permission and move your face — the brushstroke portrait updates continuously.
    3. Reload to get haunted by a different real museum painting.

    Local: python3 -m http.server in the repo root, then open localhost:8000/showcase/demos/the-enchanted-mirror/.
